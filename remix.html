Remix

For many, the idea of rip, mix, burn is an antiquated one. While still prevalent, the age of truly unoffended open source material seems to have occurred years before today. Through my work, I wish to express the value of creativity reaped from open source communities, the growing repulsion modern audiences seem to have for the art, and the barrier this creates to the development of humanity. While true enough that certain laws need to be followed when considering intellectual property, it could be even more valuable to consider the ramifications of arresting aspects of the creative mind.

“This is a kind of creativity. It is a creativity that we should remember and celebrate.” Laurence Lessig page 24

“There are some who would say that there is no creativity except this kind. We don’t need to go that far to recognize its importance.” Laurence Lessig page 24
